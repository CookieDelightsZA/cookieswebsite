Pizzelle

Ingredients:

  • 3 large eggs
  • 3/4 cup granulated sugar
  • 1/2 cup unsalted butter, melted and cooled
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 1 3/4 cups all-purpose flour
  • 2 teaspoons baking powder
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt

Instructions:

  1. In a large bowl, beat the eggs and sugar until well combined.
  2. Add the melted butter and vanilla extract to the egg mixture, and mix until smooth.
  3. In a separate bowl, whisk together the all-purpose flour, baking powder, and salt.
  4. Add the dry ingredients to the wet ingredients and mix until a smooth batter forms.
  5. Preheat the pizzelle iron according to the manufacturer's instructions.
  6. Drop about 1 tablespoon of batter onto the center of each patterned side of the preheated iron.
  7. Cook the pizzelle according to the manufacturer's instructions, usually for about 30 seconds to 1 minute, or until golden brown.
  8. Use a spatula to carefully remove the pizzelle from the iron and transfer them to a wire rack to cool completely.
  9. Repeat with the remaining batter until all the pizzelle are cooked.
  10. Enjoy these traditional Italian wafer cookies, either flat or rolled into cannoli shapes!
