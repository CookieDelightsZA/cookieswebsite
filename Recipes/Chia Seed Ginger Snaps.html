Chia Seed Ginger Snaps

Ingredients:

  • 1 1/2 cups whole wheat flour
  • 1/4 cup chia seeds
  • 1/2 cup molasses
  • 1 teaspoon ground ginger
  • 1/2 teaspoon ground cinnamon
  • 1/2 teaspoon baking soda
  • Pinch of salt

Instructions:

  1. Preheat your oven to 350°F (175°C). Line a baking sheet with parchment paper or lightly grease it.
  2. In a medium bowl, whisk together the whole wheat flour, chia seeds, ground ginger, ground cinnamon, baking soda, and a pinch of salt.
  3. In a separate bowl, warm the molasses slightly (you can microwave it for a few seconds) to make it easier to work with.
  4. Pour the molasses into the dry ingredients and mix until a cookie dough forms. If the dough is too sticky, you can add a little more flour.
  5. Using your hands, roll small portions of the dough into balls and place them on the prepared baking sheet. Flatten each ball with a fork or the back of a spoon to create a crisscross pattern.
  6. Bake for 10-12 minutes, or until the cookies are firm and the edges are lightly browned.
  7. Remove the cookies from the oven and let them cool on the baking sheet for a few minutes. Then transfer them to a wire rack to cool completely.
  8. Once the cookies are completely cool, they are ready to be enjoyed. These Chia Seed Ginger Snaps offer a delightful blend of flavors and a hint of crunch from the chia seeds.
