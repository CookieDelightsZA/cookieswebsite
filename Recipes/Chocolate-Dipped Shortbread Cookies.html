Chocolate-Dipped Shortbread Cookies

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup unsalted butter, softened
  • 1/2 cup powdered sugar
  • 2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 1 cup semisweet chocolate chips
  • 1 teaspoon vegetable shortening
  • Optional: Sprinkles, crushed nuts, or shredded coconut for decoration

Instructions:

  1. In a large mixing bowl, cream together the softened butter and powdered sugar until light and fluffy.
  2. Add the flour, salt, and vanilla extract to the butter mixture. Mix until the dough comes together and forms a smooth ball.
  3. Wrap the dough in plastic wrap and refrigerate for at least 1 hour or until firm.
  4. Preheat the oven to 350°F (175°C). Line baking sheets with parchment paper.
  5. On a lightly floured surface, roll out the chilled dough to about 1/4-inch thickness. Use cookie cutters to cut out desired shapes.
  6. Place the cookies on the prepared baking sheets and bake for 12-15 minutes or until the edges are lightly golden. Let the cookies cool on the baking sheets for a few minutes, then transfer them to a wire rack to cool completely.
  7. In a microwave-safe bowl, melt the semisweet chocolate chips and vegetable shortening together in short intervals, stirring in between, until smooth and melted.
  8. Dip one half of each cooled cookie into the melted chocolate, allowing any excess chocolate to drip off. Place the cookies back on the parchment paper.
  9. If desired, immediately sprinkle the dipped portion of the cookies with sprinkles, crushed nuts, or shredded coconut for decoration.
  10. Allow the chocolate to set completely at room temperature or place the cookies in the refrigerator for faster setting.
  11. Enjoy these delicious chocolate-dipped shortbread cookies as a delightful treat!
