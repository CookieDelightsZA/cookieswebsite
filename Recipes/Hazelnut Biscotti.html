Hazelnut Biscotti

Ingredients:

  • 2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 1/2 teaspoons baking powder
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt
  • 1/2 cup unsalted butter, softened
  • 3/4 cup granulated sugar
  • 2 large eggs
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 1 cup hazelnuts, toasted and chopped

Instructions:

  1. Preheat the oven to 350째F (175째C). Line a baking sheet with parchment paper.
  2. In a medium bowl, whisk together the all-purpose flour, baking powder, and salt.
  3. In a separate large bowl, cream together the softened butter and granulated sugar until light and fluffy.
  4. Add the eggs one at a time, beating well after each addition. Stir in the vanilla extract.
  5. Gradually add the dry ingredients to the butter mixture, mixing until a stiff dough forms.
  6. Fold in the chopped hazelnuts until evenly distributed throughout the dough.
  7. Transfer the dough to the prepared baking sheet and shape it into a 12-inch long log, about 4 inches wide.
  8. Bake for 25-30 minutes, or until the log is firm and lightly golden.
  9. Remove the log from the oven and let it cool for 10 minutes. Reduce the oven temperature to 325째F (165째C).
  10. Transfer the cooled log to a cutting board and use a serrated knife to slice it diagonally into 1/2-inch thick slices.
  11. Place the sliced cookies back on the baking sheet and bake for an additional 10-15 minutes, or until they are crispy and golden. Flip the cookies halfway through baking.
  12. Allow the biscotti to cool completely on a wire rack before serving. Enjoy the delicious Hazelnut Biscotti with your favorite hot beverage!
