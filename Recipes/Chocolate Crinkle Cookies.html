Chocolate Crinkle Cookies

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup unsweetened cocoa powder
  • 1 3/4 cups granulated sugar
  • 1/2 cup vegetable oil
  • 4 large eggs
  • 2 teaspoons vanilla extract
  • 2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 2 teaspoons baking powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1 cup powdered sugar, for rolling

Instructions:

  1. In a large bowl, whisk together the cocoa powder, granulated sugar, and vegetable oil until well combined.
  2. Add the eggs one at a time, mixing well after each addition. Stir in the vanilla extract.
  3. In a separate bowl, whisk together the all-purpose flour, baking powder, and salt.
  4. Add the dry ingredients to the cocoa mixture and stir until just combined. The dough will be thick and sticky.
  5. Cover the dough with plastic wrap and refrigerate for at least 3 hours or overnight.
  6. Preheat the oven to 350°F (175°C). Line baking sheets with parchment paper.
  7. Place the powdered sugar in a shallow bowl. Roll tablespoonfuls of dough into balls, then roll each ball in the powdered sugar until coated.
  8. Place the coated dough balls on the prepared baking sheets, spacing them about 2 inches apart.
  9. Bake for 10-12 minutes, or until the cookies have spread and the tops are crinkled. The centers will be slightly soft.
  10. Allow the cookies to cool on the baking sheets for a few minutes, then transfer them to a wire rack to cool completely.
  11. Enjoy these delightful Chocolate Crinkle Cookies with a glass of milk or your favorite hot beverage!
