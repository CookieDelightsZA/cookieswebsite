French Macarons

Ingredients:

  • 1 3/4 cups almond flour
  • 1 1/2 cups powdered sugar
  • 3 large egg whites, room temperature
  • 1/4 cup granulated sugar
  • Food coloring (optional)
  • 1/2 cup unsalted butter, softened
  • 1 cup powdered sugar
  • 1-2 tablespoons milk
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract

Instructions:

  1. In a medium bowl, sift together the almond flour and powdered sugar. Set aside.
  2. In a large mixing bowl, beat the egg whites on medium speed until foamy. Gradually add the granulated sugar and continue beating until stiff peaks form. If desired, add a few drops of food coloring and beat until combined.
  3. Gently fold the almond flour mixture into the beaten egg whites until well incorporated. The batter should have a thick, but smooth and flowing consistency.
  4. Transfer the batter to a piping bag fitted with a round tip. Pipe small circles onto parchment-lined baking sheets, spacing them about 1 inch apart.
  5. Tap the baking sheets gently on the counter to release any air bubbles. Let the piped macarons sit at room temperature for about 30 minutes to develop a skin. They should feel dry to the touch.
  6. Preheat the oven to 300°F (150°C). Bake the macarons for 15-18 minutes, or until they are set and firm. Let them cool completely on the baking sheets.
  7. In a separate bowl, cream together the softened butter, powdered sugar, milk, and vanilla extract until smooth and creamy.
  8. Transfer the filling to a piping bag fitted with a round tip. Pipe a dollop of filling onto the flat side of one macaron shell and sandwich it with another shell.
  9. Repeat with the remaining macaron shells and filling. Let the filled macarons rest in the refrigerator for at least 24 hours to develop their flavors.
  10. Enjoy these delicate and delicious French macarons as a delightful treat!
